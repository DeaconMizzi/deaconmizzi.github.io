To reflect this mission, the branding was crafted with a focus on clarity, neutrality, and inclusivity. The logo conveys perspective and balance, while the modern typography system ensures accessibility and readability across platforms. Description

Bird’s Eye View is a Maltese youth-led initiative dedicated to fostering impartial discussion and encouraging citizens to engage with current affairs beyond partisan divides. To reflect this mission, the branding was crafted with a focus on clarity, neutrality, and inclusivity. The logo conveys perspective and balance, while the modern typography system ensures accessibility and readability across platforms. A carefully chosen color palette, built around teal and contrasting tones of black and white, symbolizes openness, dialogue, and the meeting of diverse perspectives. Together, these elements establish a strong and consistent visual identity that supports Bird’s Eye View in positioning itself as a trusted, modern space for democratic debate and youth-led expression.

Concept Development

Initial wireframes created to plan the Animalia website layout, content flow, and responsiveness. These helped guide development of the homepage, character pages, and media sections.

Challenges and Insights

Designing a site for a fictional animated series introduced unique challenges:

  • Ensuring consistency between sketch concepts and real implementation
  • Balancing playful visuals with functional navigation
  • Managing responsive design for custom character layouts

Limitations & Future Improvements

While the current site successfully delivers the intended experience, there are areas to grow:

  • Adding a searchable episode guide or interactive world map
  • Expanding the news section with filtering or tagging features
  • Implementing a content management system for easier updates
