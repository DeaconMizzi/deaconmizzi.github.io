Genre: Bullet hell minigame

Goal: Dodge fireworks and survive as long as possible

Feature: Difficulty increases over time

Description

Firework Frenzy is a fast-paced bullet hell minigame where players must dodge an increasingly chaotic barrage of fireworks launched by mischievous goblins. The objective is to survive as long as possible while collecting coins to boost your score. Every 10 coins also grants a temporary shield, offering a brief moment of protection amidst the chaos.

Development Tools

Unity, VS Code, Aseprite

Challenges and Insights

One of the main challenges was managing time effectively during development, especially while fine-tuning the game’s difficulty. Early versions proved too hard for some players, requiring several balance adjustments to make the experience more accessible without losing the core tension. Art quality was another hurdle. The initial visuals didn’t meet expectations until they were reworked for a cleaner, more consistent style. Looking ahead, I plan to expand Firework Frenzy with multiple levels, enhanced particle effects, and animated cutscenes. A short narrative may also be introduced to add context and progression, along with new bullet hell mechanics to deepen gameplay and variety.
