Bird's Eye View

Bird’s Eye View is a Maltese youth-led initiative dedicated to fostering impartial discussion and encouraging citizens to engage with current affairs beyond partisan divides.

Description

To reflect this mission, the branding was crafted with a focus on clarity, neutrality, and inclusivity. The logo conveys perspective and balance, while the modern typography system ensures accessibility and readability across platforms. A carefully chosen color palette, built around teal and contrasting tones of black and white, symbolizes openness, dialogue, and the meeting of diverse perspectives. Together, these elements establish a strong and consistent visual identity that supports Bird’s Eye View in positioning itself as a trusted, modern space for democratic debate and youth-led expression.
