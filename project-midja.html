Midja

Midja is a marketing and creative agency focused on helping businesses communicate with clarity and purpose. Through web development, digital marketing, and branding, Midja creates thoughtful digital experiences that connect with audiences and drive meaningful results.

Brand Design Guidelines Visual Identity

Description

This project involved the creation of a complete brand identity and guideline system for Midja, a marketing and creative agency. The goal was to establish a clear and consistent visual language that reflects the agency’s focus on clarity, creativity, and impact. The work included logo design, colour systems, typography, and usage guidelines to ensure consistency across digital and print applications.

The identity was designed to balance structure and flexibility, allowing the brand to adapt across different contexts while maintaining a strong and recognisable presence. Emphasis was placed on simplicity, readability, and modern design principles, resulting in a system that supports both creative expression and professional communication.
